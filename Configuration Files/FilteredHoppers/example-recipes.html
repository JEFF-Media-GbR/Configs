Custom crafting recipes

You can define custom crafting recipes for the magic hopper thing. Here are a few examples:

Nautilus shell with redstone on top

recipe-shape:
  - "R"
  - "N"

recipe-ingredients:
  - "R=REDSTONE"
  - "N=NAUTILUS_SHELL"

Nautilus shell surrounded with diamonds

recipe-shape:
  - "DDD"
  - "DND"
  - "DDD"

recipe-ingredients:
  - "D=DIAMOND"
  - "N=NAUTILUS_SHELL"
